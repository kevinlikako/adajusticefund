Our Mission

The ADA Justice Fund is dedicated to ensuring equal access to digital spaces for individuals with disabilities. We advocate for compliance with accessibility standards, hold businesses accountable, and support digital inclusivity.

How We Work

  • Investigate: We analyze websites for accessibility violations.
  • Advocate: We reach out to businesses and push for compliance.
  • Resolve: We work towards fair settlements and fixes to improve web accessibility.

Recent Legal Actions & Settlements

  • Law Firm Pays $11,500 – A small legal practice agreed to a settlement after failing to provide accessible PDF documents for visually impaired clients.
  • Medical Clinic Pays $9,800 – A dental office was fined after their online appointment booking system was found inaccessible to users with screen readers.
  • E-Commerce Store Settles for $14,200 – A fashion retailer faced legal action for missing alternative text on product images, violating ADA guidelines.
  • Restaurant Chain Fined $125,000 – A popular restaurant brand settled a high-profile case after repeated failures to make their mobile ordering platform accessible.
  • Real Estate Agency Pays $12,500 – A small property listing site failed to provide text alternatives for key property details, leading to a financial settlement.
  • Local Gym Settles for $8,700 – A fitness center’s membership portal lacked keyboard navigation, prompting an ADA complaint and a settlement agreement.
  • Software Company Pays $29,500 – A growing SaaS provider agreed to compliance upgrades and a financial payout after their login portal was found incompatible with assistive technology.
  • Retailer Fined $10,400 – A boutique clothing shop reached a financial settlement after its online checkout system was flagged for ADA non-compliance.
  • National Hotel Chain Pays $48,000 – A major hospitality company resolved multiple accessibility complaints related to its online reservation system.
