Law Firm - Settled for $11,500

Violation: PDF documents on their website were unreadable by screen readers, preventing visually impaired clients from accessing legal services.

Outcome: The firm settled out of court and paid a financial settlement while committing to full ADA compliance.

E-Commerce Store - Settled for $14,200

Violation: Product images had no alternative text, making it impossible for visually impaired customers to browse products.

Outcome: The company reached a settlement agreement and made immediate accessibility changes.

Medical Clinic - Paid $9,800

Violation: Online appointment booking system was unusable with screen readers, preventing disabled patients from making medical appointments.

Outcome: After legal action, the clinic agreed to a financial settlement and site upgrades.

National Restaurant Chain - Settled for $125,000

Violation: Online ordering platform was completely inaccessible for customers with disabilities.

Outcome: Faced with a lawsuit, the company agreed to a six-figure settlement and site-wide accessibility fixes.

Hotel Chain - Paid $48,000

Violation: Booking system lacked keyboard navigation, preventing disabled users from making reservations.

Outcome: A major settlement was reached, and the company updated its website.

Law Firm - Settled for $7,000

Violation: Missing captions on informational legal videos, making content inaccessible to deaf users.

Outcome: The firm agreed to pay damages and add closed captions.
